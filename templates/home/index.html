About DeepXRAY

DeepXRAY is a cutting-edge medical imaging platform that leverages artificial intelligence to assist healthcare professionals in analyzing X-ray images. Our advanced deep learning models are trained on thousands of medical images to provide accurate, real-time analysis of chest X-rays for pneumonia detection and other abnormalities.

Built with modern web technologies and designed for healthcare professionals, DeepXRAY streamlines the diagnostic process while maintaining the highest standards of accuracy and security.
